Some variables have been recoded/ generated
self_placement: ideological self placement as asked in questions (self_eg1, self_eg2)
candidate_placement: ideological placement of preferred party as asked in questions (cand_eg1_1, can_eg1_2, can_eg2_1, can_eg2_2)
group: Experimental group; Codes: (1): Self asked first, (2): Candidate asked first
tcompl: Time in minutes it took the respondent to complete the survey (calculated from startDate and endDate)
